# Video Script Frameworks

## 15-Second Conversion Video

```text
0-2s: Pattern interrupt hook
2-5s: Pain or mistake
5-10s: Product demo
10-13s: Result, proof, or comparison
13-15s: CTA
```

## 25-Second UGC Review

```text
0-3s: Creator confession or surprising result
3-8s: Personal pain
8-16s: Demo with 2-3 visible steps
16-21s: Why it works or why it is different
21-25s: Offer and CTA
```

## Reusable Hook Types

- "I did not expect this to fix..."
- "Stop buying X before you see this."
- "This is the easiest way to..."
- "I tested the viral X so you do not have to."
- "If you struggle with X, watch this."
- "The difference after 7 days..."

## Hashtag Mix

Use 5-8 tags:

- 1-2 category tags
- 1-2 problem tags
- 1-2 audience tags
- 1-2 country or shopping intent tags
